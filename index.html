Welcome to Charleston Import Automotive

Looking for an experienced facility to Service your European car or restore your British automobile?

Charleston Import Automotive has been servicing and restoring British cars since 1989. We have over 30 years experience in servicing all British marques from pre-war through current models.If you are a member of a British Car Club, be sure to ask about our discount pricing. If you drive an Audi, Volkswagen, BMW, Mecedes, Saab, or Volvo, we can help you there as well. Our ASE Certified and trained mechanics can provide full service to major repairs for your European vehicle. As a Bosch Automotive Service Specialist we have the tools and technology on-hand to keep you safely and reliably on the road. Looking for more mileage, performance or just routine scheduled maintenance? Call us today at (843)552-6555 for suggestions or an appointment.
